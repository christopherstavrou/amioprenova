Ami Opprenova is a jazz vocalist and composer whose artistry bridges the golden age of jazz with contemporary sensibilities. Based in Sofia, Bulgaria, Ami has captivated audiences across Europe with her warm, expressive voice and sophisticated musical arrangements.

## Musical Journey

From her early years studying classical voice to discovering the improvisational freedom of jazz, Ami's musical journey has been marked by curiosity and dedication. She draws inspiration from legendary vocalists like Ella Fitzgerald and Sarah Vaughan while developing a sound distinctly her own—one that honors tradition while embracing modern production techniques.

## Performance & Recording

Ami regularly performs at prestigious jazz festivals and intimate club venues throughout Bulgaria and beyond. Her recordings, including the acclaimed albums "Eternal Melody" and "Midnight Sessions," showcase her ability to breathe new life into standards while crafting original compositions that resonate with audiences.

## Collaboration & Teaching

Beyond performing, Ami is committed to nurturing the next generation of jazz musicians. She conducts masterclasses and workshops, sharing her knowledge of vocal technique, interpretation, and the art of authentic musical expression. Her collaborative spirit has led to partnerships with talented instrumentalists and producers, constantly expanding her artistic horizons.

## Vision

Ami's vision is to create music that transcends boundaries—music that honors the past, embraces the present, and invites listeners into moments of genuine connection. Whether performing timeless standards or original compositions, she brings sincerity, technical excellence, and emotional depth to every note.
