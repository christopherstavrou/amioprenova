Many of you have asked about my production setup, so I thought I'd share some insights.

## Studio Setup

My home studio is intentionally minimal:
- Audio interface and monitors
- Microphones for vocals and instruments
- MIDI controller
- Digital audio workstation

## Production Philosophy

I believe in keeping things simple. Great music comes from ideas, not expensive gear. Focus on songwriting and arrangement first, then enhance with production.

## Workflow Tips

1. Start with a strong foundation
2. Layer thoughtfully
3. Leave space in the mix
4. Trust your instincts

Hope this helps aspiring producers out there!
