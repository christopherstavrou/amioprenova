Every artist faces creative blocks. Here's how I deal with them.

## Recognition

First, accept that blocks are normal. They're not a sign of failure – they're part of the creative journey.

## Strategies That Help

1. **Step away**: Sometimes distance provides clarity
2. **Change environment**: New spaces inspire new ideas
3. **Collaborate**: Other perspectives can unlock creativity
4. **Return to basics**: Strip everything down to essentials

## Patience and Trust

Most importantly, trust the process. Creativity ebbs and flows. The block will pass, and often you'll emerge with fresh perspective.

Don't force it. Let it flow naturally.
