One question I get asked frequently is: "Where do you find inspiration?"

## Sources of Inspiration

For me, inspiration comes from everyday life:
- Personal experiences and emotions
- Conversations with friends
- Nature and quiet moments
- Other art forms (books, films, paintings)

## The Creative Process

I don't force it. When an idea comes, I capture it quickly – voice memo, quick recording, or jotting down lyrics. Later, I refine and develop these seeds into full songs.

## Staying Open

The key is staying open and present. Inspiration is everywhere if you're paying attention.

What inspires your creativity? I'd love to hear!
